# Our Strategy and Journey

## Introduction
This document serves as a detailed record of the entire project journey, including the problems encountered, solutions implemented, and decisions made. It is designed to be read by bots, developers, or anyone who needs to understand the project's history and strategy.

---

## Project Objective
Create an integrated solution for Caldas de Tel Garage that includes:
1. A new page for the company's new brand.
2. Integration of the new page with the existing e-commerce.
3. Automation of sales and customer service through the Sofia bot.

---

## Problems Encountered and Solutions

### Problem 1: 24/7 Support for Premium Customers
- **Description**: Premium customers expect continuous support, but the garage does not operate outside business hours.
- **Solution**: Implementation of the Sofia bot for 24/7 support, with support in Portuguese and English.

### Problem 2: Integration with Existing E-commerce
- **Description**: The new brand page needs to be connected to the already implemented e-commerce system.
- **Solution**: Configuration of inventory and order synchronization between the new page and the e-commerce.

### Problem 3: Sales Automation
- **Description**: Need to automate the sales process and reporting.
- **Solution**: Configuration of the Sofia bot to manage sales, answer questions, process orders, and generate reports.
